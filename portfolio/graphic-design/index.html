16 - 18 November 2017   |   Wesleyan University

The Pillowman

Poster DesignerPublicity

Presented by the Theater Department at Wesleyan University as the Fall 2017 faculty production. Directed by Eddie Torres.

I was originally just the assistant sound designer for The Pillowman, but I convinced Eddie to let me make the poster by asking him if I could make the poster. He told me he wanted the poster to be inspired by the intro sequence to Se7en, so I initially tried to do just that and then took it from there. We workshopped the design during production meetings and everyone seemed to be happy.


9 September 2017   |   Middletown, CT

Middletown Sound

Graphic Designer

Created by Anthony Dean as the first in a series of soundscapes titled Acousmappings.

Anthony had just graduated and had a grant and funding to produce interactive soundscapes for four locations in Middletown (where Wesleyan University is located). He approached me to do all the graphic design for the project and we wanted the aesthetic to match the actual project, which consisted of funny boxes to place your head into and just listen to the soundscapes.

Watch video on project.


June - August 2017   |   NYC

The 24 Hour Plays

Graphic DesignBranding

My time with The 24 Hour Plays was extremely fun, since the organization did not have a graphic designer and was in need of a website and brand redesign. I took the initiative with both, creating a new website as well as numerous assets and a style guide. The materials I created focused on the company's productions 'The 24 Hour Plays On Broadway' and 'The 24 Hour Nationals', but I made sure my materials could be used for future productions as well.

Burying Gertrude Berg

Poster DesignerPublicity

Presented by Second Stage at Wesleyan University. Directed by Maggie Rothberg and Jordan Roe, starring and written by Jordan Roe.

My role as graphic designer for Burying Gertrude Berg was less creative, since Jordan had a couple posters ideas she really wanted to see through. As You Like It

Poster DesignerPublicity

Presented by Second Stage at Wesleyan University. Directed by Dan Bachman.

I did not realize I was going to be the graphic designer for this show, as I was initially asked to just quickly create an audition poster. The team liked my design so much they actually altered the set design to match it, and asked me back for the final show poster.


June 2016 - January 2017   |   NYC

Iridium Jazz Club

Graphic Design

I started at Iridium as a club-based intern, working daily operations in the restaurant and ticketing areas. At some point I was asked to help with administrative tasks that included extensive scanning and editing of legal forms, and I expedited the process such that it took several days fewer to complete than expected. From there, I was brought on to do more graphic projects under the supervision of the club director and graphic designer, and I experimented with consistent branding and aesthetic styles during my duration with the company.
