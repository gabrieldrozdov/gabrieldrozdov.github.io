I work with theater and arts organizations to develop and shape identity systems. I step in to evaluate preexisting brands and analyze their key characteristics to design modernized updates.

My passion is to help organizations that have limited access to high-quality graphic and web design due to financial barriers. In every project, I explore ways to minimize cost and reinvigorate the company's confidence in its public image. In my designs, I prioritize sustainability and ease-of-implementation to make something beautiful and practical.

I am also an avid theatrical sound designer, composer, and music producer! I love to write music for piano as well as push the technological and creative limits for sound in theater.
