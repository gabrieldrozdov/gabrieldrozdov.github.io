Hello! My name is Gabriel Drozdov. I'm a graphic, web, and sound designer double majoring in Computer Science and Theater at Wesleyan University. I also identify as a writer, composer, painter, video game enthusiast, organization freak, and digital proponent.



This summer, I'm working as the Membership & Creative Affairs intern at The Dramatists Guild. I'm providing technical assistance to our membership database and CRM, designing and developing a promotional video, and creating numerous graphic assets alongside a company style guide.



My current project is a senior thesis in theatrical sound design, No Replica. To develop this piece, I am completing numerous readings on theatrical sound design, performance studies, and experimental music/performance. In addition to the piece, I will write a 40-60 page scholarly paper.
