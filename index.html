Making great design more accessible.

Helping artists with graphic, web, and sound design. See what I've done

I am a graphic, web, & sound designer whose passion is helping theater and arts organizations develop and shape identity systems. Through design, organizations can find platforms to voice their missions and reach greater audiences more effectively and efficiently.

I studied at Wesleyan University, where I double-majored in Computer Science & Theater. Wesleyan brought me into the theater community and gave me the privilege of producing and working on 50 student productions as a member of our student theater company, Second Stage. I used theater as a way to hone my creative skills and engage in dialogues on diversity and inclusivity both at Wesleyan and beyond.

My academic and professional work nurtured my interest in design—I believe my voice and experience is best fit for helping others find a platform and voice for themselves. I live to see new perspectives succeed on and off stages.
